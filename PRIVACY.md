# Privacy Policy for Banco de Alimentos

**Last updated:** March 13, 2026

## 1. Overview
Banco de Alimentos ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a browser extension designed to help users monitor product availability and manage cart operations on the Banco de Alimentos de Costa Rica website. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## 2. Data Collection
We **do not** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, browsing history, or usage analytics to our own servers. The extension runs entirely locally on your device.

## 3. Data Storage
Banco de Alimentos does not use any persistent storage. All state (monitoring status, product counts) is held in memory and discarded when the browser tab is closed.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The extension communicates **only** with the Banco de Alimentos de Costa Rica website (`bancodealimentos.or.cr`) to:
- Fetch the shop page to check current product availability.
- Send add-to-cart requests on behalf of the user.

No data is sent to any other third-party service, analytics provider, or remote server.

## 5. Permissions
The extension requires host permissions for `bancodealimentos.or.cr` only. It does not access any other websites.

## 6. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the support section on the Chrome Web Store.
